#Countly Roadmap

###**DRAFT**

Countly team is committed to maximizing the usefullness of Countly platform, creating a complete, user-oriented and extendible application analytics framework primarily for mobile devices (although SDKs exist for Mac OS X and Roku devices). This page is intended to provide a basic roadmap of where we are heading as we work on enhancements, fixes and improvements on major functional and architectural parts. ##1. Short-term focus

##2. Medium-term focus

* Appstore/Google Play connectors
* Feedback reporting
* Push notifications
